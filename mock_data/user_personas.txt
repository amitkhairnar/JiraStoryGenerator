# User Personas

Persona: Project Manager (Admin User)
- Needs: To see project status, budget, and timelines at a glance.
- Goals: Ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget.
- Technical Skills: Low. Prefers simple interfaces and clear dashboards.

Persona: Standard User (Team Member)
- Needs: To view assigned tasks, update their status, and collaborate with team members.
- Goals: Complete their work efficiently.
- Technical Skills: Varies. Generally comfortable with standard web applications.

Persona: Unregistered Visitor
- Needs: To understand what the product does and how to sign up.
- Goals: Evaluate the service and create an account if interested.
- Technical Skills: N/A.
